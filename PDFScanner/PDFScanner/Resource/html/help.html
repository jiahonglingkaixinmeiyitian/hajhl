How to scan?

Tap the "📸" button;

Aim your camera at the paper document. The Scanner App will detect borders and take a photo automatically;

Tap "Continue";

Tap "Done" when you finish scanning.

How to edit documents?

Tap a document and find the available features:

  • Rename the document (Open a document -> Tap the current name to rename);
  • Add a new page to the document by tapping the ‎"➕" after thumbnail images;
  • Edit the document: adjust borders, brightness, and contrast;
  • Sign the document;
  • Extract text with OCR 
How to adjust borders?

Open the document -> Tap "Edit", select:

  • Manually adjust borders by dragging;
  • Change it to full screen or reset back to the initial border;
  • Rotate document;
  • When you have finished adjusting, tap “Continue” and “Done” to save settings.
How to share a document?

Open the document -> Tap “Share” -> Select “Share with PNG” or “Share with PDF” -> Share through Text, Email, Facebook, LinkedIn, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp and other sharing options.
